# Codify LMS - Quick Start Guide

## How to Login & Switch Roles

### Quick Login (Recommended for Demo)

On the login page, you'll see three quick login buttons at the bottom:

1. **Login as Student** (Green dot) - Access student dashboard
2. **Login as Faculty** (Teal dot) - Access faculty dashboard  
3. **Login as Admin** (Purple dot) - Access admin dashboard

Simply click any of these buttons to instantly log in with that role.

### How to Logout

1. Click on your **avatar/profile** button in the top-right corner
2. A dropdown menu will appear showing:
   - Your name and email
   - Your current role (Student/Faculty/Admin)
   - Settings option
   - **Logout option**
3. Click **"Logout"** 
4. You'll see a success message: "Logged out successfully"
5. You'll be redirected back to the login page

### Switching Between Roles

To switch from one role to another:

1. **Logout** from your current session (see above)
2. On the login page, click one of the **quick demo login buttons** for a different role
3. You'll be logged in with the new role and see the appropriate dashboard

## Troubleshooting

### Can't logout?
- Make sure you're clicking the **avatar in the top-right corner**
- The logout option is in the dropdown menu that appears
- If you don't see it, try refreshing the page

### Logged out but still seeing old dashboard?
- Clear your browser cache
- The page should automatically redirect to login after logout
- If not, manually refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)

### Want to switch roles quickly?
- No need to log out manually each time
- Just use the quick login buttons on the login page
- They will override your current session
